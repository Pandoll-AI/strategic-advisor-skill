# Research Quality Rubric

Score each serious research output from 0 to 3.

| Dimension | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Scope | Vague | Basic topic | Clear question | Clear question + boundaries |
| Source quality | Unsupported | Mostly weak | Mix of decent sources | Primary/authoritative sources dominate |
| Freshness | Ignored | Some dates | Dates considered | Current facts actively verified |
| Contradictions | Ignored | Mentioned vaguely | Some conflict surfaced | Actively searched and explained |
| Claim-level verification | None | Informal | Key claims checked | Atomic claims with status/evidence |
| Decision usefulness | Generic | Some relevance | Clear implications | Recommendation + actions + gates |
| Uncertainty | Hidden | Vague caveats | Stated limitations | Confidence and next verification steps |

A release-quality Strategic Advisor synthesis should score at least 15/21, with no 0s on source quality, freshness, or claim verification when factual claims drive the recommendation.
